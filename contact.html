Before You Submit

Please ensure your article:

  • Is original and not published elsewhere
  • Is written in clear, accessible English
  • Cites primary sources appropriately
  • Includes a short abstract (150–300 words)
  • Declares any conflicts of interest
Formats Accepted
  • HTML Preferred — ready to publish
  • Markdown Converted by the team
  • Google Docs Share a view link
Review Timeline

Publication: within 1 week of final acceptance
