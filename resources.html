Resources

Curated tools, databases, textbooks, and communities for biosystems research.

🗄️ Databases & Repositories

  • 🧬
    NCBI National Center for Biotechnology Information — sequences, literature, and more.
  • 🌐
    UniProt Comprehensive, high-quality protein sequence and functional information database.
  • 🔗
    STRING Protein–protein interaction networks for systems-level analysis.
  • 📊
    Expression Atlas Gene and protein expression data across species, tissues, and conditions (EMBL-EBI).
  • 🌿
    GBIF Global Biodiversity Information Facility — open access to species occurrence data.
  • ⚡
    BioModels Repository of mathematical models of biological processes (EMBL-EBI).

🛠️ Tools & Software

  • 🐍
    Biopython Python tools for computational molecular biology — parsing, analysis, and more.
  • 📈
    Bioconductor Open-source R packages for bioinformatics and genomic data analysis.
  • 🔬
    Cytoscape Open-source platform for visualising and analysing complex biological networks.
  • 🧪
    Tellurium Python environment for modelling and simulating biochemical and biosystems networks.
  • 📐
    COPASI COmplex PAthway SImulator — biochemical network simulation and analysis.

📰 Open Access Journals

  • 📄
    PLOS Biology Peer-reviewed, open-access biological research across all scales of life.
  • 📄
    eLife Non-profit open-access journal for outstanding advances in life and biomedical sciences.
  • 📄
    bioRxiv Free preprint server for the biological sciences — immediate open access to findings.
  • 📄
    PubMed Central Free full-text archive of biomedical and life sciences literature.

🎓 Online Courses & Lectures

  • 🎓
    iBiology Free video lectures from leading biologists on a wide range of life science topics.
  • 🎓
    MIT OpenCourseWare — Biology Free course materials from MIT's biology, systems biology, and computational biology courses.
  • 🎓
    Systems Biology (Coursera) Icahn School of Medicine at Mount Sinai specialisation in systems biology.
